About the Author

Hadiza Bagudu

A talented author and poet with a degree in International Studies from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria. As a Microsoft Certified Professional residing in Abuja with her husband and two children, Hadiza weaves emotions into poetic verses, unveiling hidden narratives of the soul.

Her collection "The Quiet Parts" explores themes of nature, love, faith, and introspection—inviting readers to find serenity in the spaces between words and discover truths that bloom in silence.
